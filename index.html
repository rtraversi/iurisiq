Confidentiality First

Your Clients' Secrets Stay Yours.

Our AI architecture is designed from the ground up around one principle: your clients' information never leaves your controlled environment. Unlike generic AI tools, IurisIQ operates in an isolated, single-tenant deployment where the AI works with your data — and only for your firm.

01
Isolated Tenant Architecture

Your firm's database, storage, and AI context are completely separated from any other client. There is no shared pool — ever.

02
AI That Doesn't Learn From Your Data

Your documents serve as AI context. They are never used to retrain or improve any external model. Your knowledge stays yours.

03
Database-Enforced Access Control

Role permissions are enforced at the database row level. Even a misconfigured UI can't expose data it shouldn't see.

04
Malware Scanning on Every Upload

Every file — from clients or staff — is scanned by a commercial antivirus engine before it's visible. Infected files are deleted automatically.

05
Nightly Encrypted Backups

Your data is backed up nightly to encrypted offsite storage with a tested, documented restore procedure. We've run the drill.

06
Two-Factor Authentication

Attorneys and firm owners are required to enroll in TOTP-based 2FA — a time-based code from their authenticator app, every sign-in. Support staff can opt in; client portal access can be enabled as well. A stolen password alone can never open the portal.
